---
title: Software Developer Intern at Location Solutions, LLC
---

From March to August 2024, I worked as a part-time remote Software Developer Intern
at Location Solutions, LLC, based out of Dubai.

This was my first professional experience — and what an experience it was.

Location Solutions was a small business with close to 100 employees across all
departments. As far as I'm aware, the tech team was compact, with just 5 people.

I was onboarded by the team lead, who gave me context about what the company did
and walked me through what I'd be working on over the coming months: a revamp of a
legacy application that was slow and in need of new features.

I started by going through the existing application to understand everything a user
could currently do with it. From there, I came up with a set of features that could
be added alongside the existing requirements, compiled them into a complete feature
list, and presented it to the team, receiving a positive response.

With a UI design from the design team in hand, I moved into high-level and low-level
system design. Once that was approved, I completed the implementation.

Before rolling it out for general availability, I presented the application to a
group of internal teammates selected as pilot users. They used it, liked it, and
offered a few minor UX improvement suggestions.

It was deeply satisfying to see my hard work translate into a noticeably better
experience for the application's users.

I then contributed to another project before my time at Location Solutions came to
an end. Working with the agile team had been a genuinely enjoyable experience, and
it brought with it both technical and interpersonal growth.
