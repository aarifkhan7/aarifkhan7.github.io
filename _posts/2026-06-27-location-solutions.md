title: Software Developer Intern at Location Solutions, LLC
---

From March to August 2024 I worked as a part-time remote Software Developer Intern at Location Solutions, LLC, based out of Dubai.

This was my first professional experience, and if I recall what an experience it was.

Location Solution was a small business with close to 100 employees across all departments, as far as I am aware the tech team was organic with 5 people.

I was onboarded by the team lead, and after given some context about what the company does.
After that he shared what I was to work on in the upcoming months, It was a revamp of a legacy application, which was slow, and needed some new features.

I quickly went through the existing services, to understand what all a user can do right now with it.
