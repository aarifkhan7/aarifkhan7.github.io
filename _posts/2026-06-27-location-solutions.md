---
title: Software Developer Intern at Location Solutions, LLC
---

From March to August 2024 I worked as a part-time remote Software Developer Intern at Location Solutions, LLC, based out of Dubai.

This was my first professional experience, and if I recall what an experience it was.

Location Solution was a small business with close to 100 employees across all departments, as far as I am aware the tech team was organic with 5 people.

I was onboarded by the team lead, and was given some context about what the company does.
After that he shared what I was to work on in the upcoming months, It was a revamp of a legacy application, which was slow, and needed some new features.

I quickly went through the existing application, to understand what all a User could do right now with it, and came with some features that could be added to the new revamp, along with the current requirements. I presented the complete feature list to my team, and got a positive review.

I was provided with an UI design from the design team.

I then begin with a high and low level system design and once it was approved, completed the implemenatation.

I presented the application to my internal teammates who were selected as pilot users, before rolling out for general availability. They used the application and liked it, along with some minor user experience improvement suggestions.

It provided a good sense of accomplishment to see my hardwork bring forth ease in the application users.

I then contributed to another project, before my time at Location Solutions came to an end.

I had really enjoyed working with the agile team, and it bought forth both technical and interpersonal learnings.
